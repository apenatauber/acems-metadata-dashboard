ACEMS Metadata HQ

Welcome to the ACEMS Metadata HQ. Use this app to explore ACEMS call data from Fall 2016 to Spring 2022. Two visualization options are available:

  • Map view: An interactive map of Amherst College campus comparing call amounts at different locations. Data can be filtered by academic year, semester, call category, and chief complaint, and both total call counts and percentages can be shown. Percentages are taken as
    calls in selected category and chief complaint
    divided by
    total calls in selected academic year and semester, in that location.

  • Bar chart: Horizontal bar chart visualizing call amounts from month to month and semester to semester. Data can be colored by nature of call, category, shift type, weekend/weekday, and result; and it can be filtered by academic year, month, and semester. The bar chart is interactive: items in the legend can be clicked on to remove them from the display and double-clicked on to remove all other elements.

No patient-identifying information is available in this web app. Data is taken from the ACEMS metadata spreadsheet, which collects responses manually from a Google form filled out by the responding team.

Some useful acronyms and abbreviations:

  • AFD: Amherst Fire Department
  • AHS: Amherst Health Services (Keefe Health Center)
  • CDH: Cooley Dickinson Hospital
  • EtOH: Abbreviation for the chemical structure of alcohol. Used in EMS settings to refer to consumption of alcohol.
  • UHS: University Health Services (UMass)
